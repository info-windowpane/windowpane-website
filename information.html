windowpane Information Projects

Windowpane is a project space and curatorial experiment where temporary postcard installations become studies in themes of light, display (and concealment), puzzles, and communication.

Twenty plastic sleeves are fixed to a window. In each sleeve is a postcard. Each postcard, different from its neighbour, is acting as a part of a whole. This whole is Windowpane. The postcards are changed regularly, and after their time on the window they are posted to friends, family, collaborators, in order to spark new connections and projects.
